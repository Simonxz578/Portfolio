# Publication notes

These notes record the conservative content decisions used for the first portfolio release.

## Included

- Physics-to-applied-AI-to-adoption narrative.
- Public-safe Hirain model, RAG and agent-workflow scope.
- Cambridge AI-policy research framing, without participant data or findings.
- UCL teacher-confidence research, labelled as a manuscript under revision following a major-revision decision.
- Cambridge CEO and Formal Hall Networking Society leadership, without claims of University endorsement.
- Public LinkedIn profile supplied in the supplemental leadership material.
- Public-safe leadership operations: programme planning, stakeholder coordination, event workflow design, committee responsibilities and succession documentation.
- The society archive’s verified front-end stack and Simon’s supplied account of his website-development contribution.

## Deliberately excluded pending evidence

- Rankings, predicted grades and cohort-position claims.
- Efficiency, audience, event, code-volume, paper-volume and teaching-volume metrics.
- Confidential employer code, data, documents, architecture and commercial outcomes.
- Research sample sizes, findings and publication claims not supported by supplied material.
- Mobile number, WeChat details and source-document metadata.
- Instagram and Chinese social-media profiles, which are not needed for the professional English portfolio.
- The supplemental 100,000+ reach, all-college coordination and cross-university partnership claims, which lack sufficient independent evidence.
- The supplemental “present” end date for the founding presidency, which conflicts with the documented 2026–2027 succession; the bounded 2025–2026 period is retained.
- GitHub, repository, demo and DOI links that were not supplied.
- A downloadable résumé PDF, which requires a separate approved public file.
